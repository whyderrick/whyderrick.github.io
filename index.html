Who's Derrick?

I am! I'm a writer and bit whisperer, based out of Oakland, California. If you're asking, "what is a bit whisperer other than a term you just made up?" you're asking the right questions.

So What Do You Do?

I work as a web developer at thoughtbot, where I build custom web applications and help clients define their products and strengthen their software engineering culture.

I'm also a poet. I read and perform around the Bay and try to make make the liteary community around here more vibrant. I read for Muzzle Magazine as a Senior Poetry Reader. I'm a 2016 alum of the Kearny Street Workshop's Interdisciplianry Writer's Lab.

When I'm not programming or writing, you can catch me dipping into comic books, inventive text novels, or video games. Basically, catch me wherever someone is trying to imagine a new world.
